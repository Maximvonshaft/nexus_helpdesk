# Supersessions and Tombstones

Tombstones make obsolete, fixed, contradicted, or replaced conclusions non-current without deleting audit history.

Every record must identify:

- the exact subject and prior finding or state;
- the previous and new status;
- the exact evidence that caused the transition;
- the current `main` SHA and governance commit;
- the lane authorized to issue the transition.

Tombstones cannot manufacture proof. A disputed finding remains `REVALIDATION_REQUIRED` until independently resolved.
