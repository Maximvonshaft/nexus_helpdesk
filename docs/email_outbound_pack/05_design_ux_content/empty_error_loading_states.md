# Empty / Error / Loading States

## Email unavailable

Title: Email is not ready  
Body: This ticket cannot use email yet.  
Details: list `missing[]` from capability API.  
Action: Ask an admin to configure the email account or add a customer email.

## Email account missing

Title: Email account missing  
Body: No active email sender is configured for this market.  
Action: Configure an Email Channel Account.

## Customer email missing

Title: Customer email missing  
Body: Add a valid customer email before sending.

## Provider send failed

Title: Email failed to send  
Body: The message was not accepted by the email provider.  
Action: Check the failure reason and retry when fixed.

## Bounce received

Title: Email bounced  
Body: The recipient email address may be invalid or unavailable.  
Action: Confirm the customer's email before sending again.

## Complaint received

Title: Complaint received  
Body: This customer marked the email as spam.  
Action: Do not resend without manager review.
