# Business Readiness Checklist

- [ ] Support agents trained on Email send flow.
- [ ] Support lead trained on bounce/complaint handling.
- [ ] Admin knows how to configure email account.
- [ ] DevOps knows rollback command.
- [ ] Test mailbox available.
- [ ] First production rollout limited to controlled scope.
- [ ] Incident escalation channel defined.
- [ ] Customer communication policy approved.
